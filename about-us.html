Who We Are?

Entrepreneurship Cell (E-Cell) is a non-profit organisation run by the students of CT University, Ludhiana. We create awareness among students about Entrepreneurship through our various programs like workshops, speaker sessions and other such events. We support the upcoming Entrepreneurs by providing them necessary resources such mentors, consultancy, seed fund and networking.
